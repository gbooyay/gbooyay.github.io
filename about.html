I'm currently in my final year at The University of Texas at Austin, double majoring in physics and astronomy. In addition to my major fields of study, I'm also earning my Certificate in Scientific Computation and Data Sciences. Read more about my past and present projects here.

Although this project lays outside the scope my other research, through it I have developed my computational skills and gained insight into the changing landscape of social media and today's modern world.
